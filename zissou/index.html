About

Your Dive Center on the Pacific Coast

For over 20 years, Zissou Divers has been introducing travelers to the underwater world of Zihuatanejo Bay. We are a PADI 5-Star and SSI certified dive center serving the Pacific coast of Guerrero, Mexico.

Founded by divers who fell in love with the reefs of Guerrero, Zissou Divers has grown from a small beach operation into a full-service dive center with private dock access, modern equipment, and a team of certified instructors. We know every rock, every crevice, and every sea turtle on this coast.

Located at Paseo del Morro in Zihuatanejo, we offer easy access to the best dive sites in the region including El Morro, Los Pelicanos, La Ropa reef, and the famous Morros de Potosi. Our facility features private parking, showers, bathrooms, and an outdoor table area for relaxing between dives.

The Pacific coast offers a different ecosystem from the Caribbean. Here you will find cooler currents, richer plankton, larger marine life, and dramatic rock formations. We operate with a strict safety-first philosophy, small groups, bilingual instruction, and personalized service that sets us apart.
