The Dive Center

Fonatur Marina, Cozumel, Quintana Roo, Mexico. PADI 5-Star Dive Center with 9+ years of excellence.

Scuba Life Cozumel was born from a simple belief: the best diving experiences come from small groups, expert guides, and genuine hospitality. Located at Fonatur Marina on Cozumel's stunning southern coast, we have built a reputation as the island's most trusted dive operation.

Our philosophy is straightforward. We limit every group to a maximum of 4 divers per guide. We extend bottom times to 70 minutes because we believe in diving your tank, not the clock. Every piece of rental equipment is Scubapro -- maintained to the highest standard. Our boat carries a high-pressure oxygen tank for every trip, because safety is never optional.

With 2,470+ certifications issued across every PADI level from Open Water to Divemaster, we have introduced hundreds of divers to the underwater world and helped thousands more explore Cozumel's legendary reefs.
